how to play

This is a quiz to test your knowledge on country flags, capital cities and populations from around the world!

To begin, select a region of the world you want to play with, and choose a difficulty setting.

Playing on Easy mode will only ask you questions about major countries with very large populations... whilst playing on Expert mode will include all countries, including micro-populations as small as 30 people!

Each correct answer is worth 5 points, and you score a bonus 5 points if you correctly answer all 3 questions on a single country!

All country data for this quiz is pulled from the REST Countries API, with the full list of countries available HERE

If you experience any errors or incorrect answers, please report them here. your feedback will help continually improve the game experience for all players!

Have fun, learn lots and good luck!!
